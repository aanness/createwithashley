About Me

I graduated with a degree in Visual Communication Design from the University of Akron. I am currently learning Full Stack Web Development at Case Western Reserve. I am an accomplished marketing professional with ten years of marketing, communications, branding and web development experience. I have worked in a variety of industries which has helped me develop a well-balanced set of skills along with a diverse work experience.
